About Sloth Pilates


A place for you to slow down


Our studio located in a small town where people don't have much resources and facilities to attend a Pilates class. When I finished my first instructor training and started teaching in Taipei 10 years ago, I have seen many of my clients get benefit from Pilates exercises. Hence, I decided to bring it back to my families and hometown.

Our Mission: We believe Pilates should be accessible to all. Our serene studio spaces are designed to help you slow down, become more present and feel more connected through breath and movement. We provide a modern and contemporary Pilates approach, deeply rooted in anatomy and biomechanics. Our classes will help you find strength, balance, proper alignment, and leave you feeling longer, stronger, energized and rejuvenated. Come to join us.


About Pilates: Pilates was created by Joseph Pilates in early 1900 and getting more and more popular nowadays. Many people are obsessed with Pilates exercises as the focus is on developing the safest and most effective fitness programming. The equipments, such as reformers, cadillac, chairs, and barrels are designed for everyone. The beginners would find them help mobilize spine & limbs and increase self-awareness from inside out; the intermediate or advanced levels would find them challenging and fun.
